Language


This is a daily planner for learning a new language of your choice. This calendar gives you suggestions on how much time to spend studying the language everyday and gives you activities to improve your skills in reading, writing, and speaking the language. If you click on the activity, you are able to copy it and add it to your own Google calendar. If you want the entire Google calendar, you can also download it and add it to your own google calendar. Feel free to choose the activities that work best for you and customize the calendar to fit your schedule.
