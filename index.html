Mission Statement

To challenge the way society sees free time and encourage ourselves to learn and grow.


Vision Statement

To inspire and help people reach their full potential.


Inspiration

We were inspired to create this website because we wanted to give people opportunities to be able to use their time in quarantine productively. During quarantine many of us were very bored and weren't sure how we should spend our time. Studies have shown that boredom is one of the main emotions felt during quarantine and that it is detrimental to mental health. Boredom also is the leading reason behind people breaking the stay-at-home order. Our team faced the facts and our plight. Then, an idea struck and we had the thought to create this website.



Our Website

We are a website that offers you many ideas and resources to help you out when you are bored. These include fitness challenges, fun activities, music recommendations, recipes, and much more. We also have downloadable month-long challenges which give you daily activities. Be sure to check out our personalized page where we give you custom recommendations based on your interests. Have a great time browsing our site, we hope you're lifted out of boredom!
